About

Dmitrii Ishutin is the founder of Ram Sky and a senior independent contractor available for interim and programme leadership engagements across power markets, BESS commercialisation, and trading transformation.

His background spans EDF Energy (curve trader, £10m P&L), Shell (product lead on the Battery Trading Platform underpinning a £20m+ revenue BESS portfolio that topped the Modo Energy leaderboard for two consecutive months), and Brady Technologies (quant analytics manager on PowerDesk). Recent clients include Statera Energy and VPI.

Engagement types

Available for senior contracting roles across the trading transformation lifecycle. Comfortable leading hybrid teams of internal staff, third-party developers, and other contractors.

  • Programme Director / Programme Manager, covering trading transformation, ETRM implementation, and route-to-market build
  • Interim Head of Trading / Head of Quant, for IPPs, suppliers, and asset owners
  • Project Lead, covering BESS commercialisation, exchange registration, BSC accession, and forecasting builds
  • ETRM Implementation Lead / Senior Business Analyst, covering vendor selection, requirements gathering, integration design, data mapping, UAT, and go-live for trading platforms
  • Sales Engineering and pre-sales, for ETRM vendors and trading SaaS providers

Hands-on, not oversight-only

Engagements range from full programme leadership down to rolling up sleeves on a model, a platform spec, or an algorithm. Most clients hire the combination, a programme lead who can also build, debug, and trade.

This is rare in the contractor market and is the most common reason engagements extend beyond their original scope.
